About me

I am David, currently studying Computer Science @ UCC. My hobbies include photography, travel, digital design and of course... programming. I have enjoyed working with computers (both hardware and software) since I was 11. After being so intrigued by how computers work, I decided to learn the know-how of programming starting with python. Being born in Hungary and growing up in Ireland made appreciate the cultural differences, this both engaged my interest in travel and photography alike in order to share and exhibit pictures to others, in catagories like nature and architecture.
